Join the Institute

I welcome motivated students and researchers interested in developing and applying Causal Artificial Intelligence to tackle challenging problems across biomedical and broader life sciences.

If you are interested in joining the Institute as a HiWi (student assistant), or would like to discuss the supervision of a Bachelor's or Master's thesis related to causal AI, causal discovery and inference, explainable AI, or related topics, please feel free to get in touch.

Together, we explore how causal approaches can enable more reliable, interpretable, and mechanistically grounded AI methods for complex biological and biomedical systems. Possible research directions include causal discovery, causal inference, uncertainty quantification, multimodal biomedical data analysis, causal representation learning, and the integration of domain knowledge into AI models.
